SHARPS builds on decades of investment into VLTI infrastructure and utilises technology breakthroughs in the following areas to open the chapter of sub-milli-arcsecond astronomy at VLTI:

- **Ultra-low noise detectors:** APD detector technology enables now low dark current as well as large pixel formats, making them near-perfect science detectors for SHARPS.

- **Astrophotonics:** Beam combination and wavefront sensing can now be facilitated more efficiently than possible before, promising further gains in sensitivity.

- **Advanced wavefront control:** Innovative methods of wavefront control improve both the light injection, and the contrast achievable at smaller inner working angles.

- **High-efficiency gratings with wide wavelength coverage:** Volume Phase Holographic gratings with multiplexing enable high efficiency, as well as wide wavelength coverage.
